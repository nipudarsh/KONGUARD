# Code of Conduct

We aim to keep this project welcoming, professional, and harassment-free.

## Expectations
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Provide evidence when reporting bugs.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or threats
- Personal attacks or abusive language
- Publishing private information

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove content or contributors who violate this policy.
